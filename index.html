No. Our process is designed to be more flexible than traditional rental or dealership options. We work with drivers across different credit situations.
Can I use a debit card or CashApp?
Yes. Visa and Mastercard debit cards are accepted. CashApp works if linked to a Visa card with sufficient balance.
Can I use the car for work or rideshare (Uber/Lyft)?
Yes. Many of our vehicles are suitable for Uber, Lyft, and other work-related use. Just let us know when you reach out and we'll match you to the right vehicle.
How fast can I get a car?
Most customers are able to book and pick up the same day. Get in touch and we'll confirm availability and walk you through the next steps immediately.
I'm not comfortable with apps — can someone help me?
Absolutely. Our team can guide you step by step over the phone. Just call us and a real person will help you get set up quickly.
